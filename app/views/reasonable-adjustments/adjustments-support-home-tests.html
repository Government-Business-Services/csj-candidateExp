Adjustments and support

We want to make sure that our application process is fair to everyone.

If you feel there are reasonable adjustments to the application process that we could make, tell us below.

Examples of reasonable adjustments include:

  • providing interview questions before the actual interview
  • letting you have more time to complete tests or assessments
  • giving you an accessible interview room if you are on crutches or recovering from an operation
This is not an exhaustive list and we will consider any adjustments or support that you ask for.

You can also tell us if there's some information that you think might be useful for us to know. For example, you could tell us if you do not like to make eye contact with a person who's interviewing you and we would pass this information on to the person doing the interview.

! Warning Telling us about any adjustments or support does not affect how we assess your application in any way. You will not be penalised for asking for support or an adjustment.
