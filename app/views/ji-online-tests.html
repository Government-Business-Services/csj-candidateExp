Judgement test

You have been invited to complete the Civil Service Judgement online test.

You’ll need to pass this test before you can progress to the next step in your application.

The Judgement test has 3 sections and will take 45 minutes to complete.

Section 3 is timed and will be a set of questions.

Before you start

How to prepare

To prepare for the test, you can:

  • read our help on preparing for the online tests

  • read the test guide

  • take the Judgement practice test

What you need

We recommend you taking the test on a large screen (a laptop or desktop).

For the test, you will need:

  • good internet connection

  • time with no interruptions

  • to take the test alone. Your application could be withdrawn if any evidence of receiving help is found
