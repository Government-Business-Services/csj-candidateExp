Your Civil Service.

What we offer

An incredible range of jobs

You could be working as a vet, a customer service adviser, a weapons engineer, a marine biologist or a web developer, to name a few. Whatever you do, and wherever you work, a job in the Civil Service is an opportunity to make a difference and be recognised for your efforts.

See the jobs and professions available to you

A great work-life balance

Flexible, hybrid and part-time working. Job sharing, at least 25 days annual leave and family-focused support including maternity, paternity and adoption leave. Even special leave for emergencies or caring. The Civil Service can help you work in a way that works for you.

Find out more

A diversive and inclusive team

The Civil Service is a modern and diverse workplace committed to ensuring equality. We embrace the different backgrounds and opinions our staff bring, using them to promote fairness within our organisations and to better serve our communities.

See how the Civil Service plans to make everyone feel welcome

An excellent pension

Join one of the best workplace pension schemes in the UK public sector. For every 5.45% you contribute, the Civil Service will add a further 27.1%.

Listen to our podcast on what's so good about the Civil Service pension

Opportunities to develop and thrive

We want to empower you to meet your career goals. You can get comprehensive support through training, mentoring, secondments and talent programmes like Fast Stream.

See how we can help you achieve your career dreams

Wellbeing support in and out of work

We help you feel healthy and supported, by giving you access to discount gym memberships, retail discounts, season ticket and bike loans, and support during any times of stress or difficulty.

Find out more about the help we have for you
