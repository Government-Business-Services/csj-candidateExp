At this point, the candidate will have been sent an email advising them an interview has been offered to them.

The email may advise logging into their candidate portal to view the details. The candidate may also see this version of their portal if they log in after an interview has been offered but before they have actioned that offer further (i.e. booked an interview or withdrawn their application).
