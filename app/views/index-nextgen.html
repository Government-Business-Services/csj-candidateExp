(3) Candidate applies
Event Who Status Testing notes
Candidate signs in or registers
Notes
If no existing account cookie is detected, we show this page as we assume no account exists.

If a cookie is found, this version with alternate content is shown.

Applying is not the only place where a candidate might sign in or register - there are a number of entry points, including saving a job, creating a job alert etc (mainly any action that is specific to that candidate and needs to be stored in their account). A candidate may also choose to register in preparation for one of these actions, without actually saving a job, applying etc at that time.

It is also possible that a candidate might choose not to sign in or register until they have decided they meet the eligibility criteria for a role.
candidate TESTED Two rounds. No users were keen on using social media profiles to sign in, as they felt personal and application log ins should be kept separate. There were also some issues with spotting how to create an account if they didn't already have one.

Notes
The portal - or user account page - is the central hub for a candidate when they sign in or create an account. From here, the candidate can manage their account, applications, saved jobs, job alerts and more.
